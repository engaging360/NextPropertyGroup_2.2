Imagine a beachside address boasting an immense 280m2 residence without the maintenance and upkeep. These soon to be constructed duplexes with a distinct focus on quality and lifestyle are positioned one street from beautiful Warana Beach and are larger than the average home.

Each with 4 bedrooms, 2.5 bathrooms and multiple living zones plus media room – space for the family is well and truly accommodated for. Quintessential Queensland living is on show with North facing outdoor living zones featuring private swimming pools to both units and tiled alfresco complete with outdoor kitchen with built in BBQs. The easy, functional floor plan flows freely from the entrance of each unit with the main living area, dining and kitchen and butler’s pantry. The media room, powder room and family sized laundry also share the lower level of the units. The upper level is home to the 4 bedrooms and an additional living area which provides a nice balance and separation between communal zones.

Finished to the highest standard with Bosch appliances, ducted air conditioning, 2pac cabinetry and floor to ceiling tiles in the bathrooms, these duplexes are a beachside dream. With commencement of construction imminent and projects of this calibre a rarity, do not wait to secure this address for yourself.

We are pleased to present to the market a rare opportunity to purchase this classic style 6,640m2 Estate framed by lush, leafy rainforest in the dress circle pocket of Forest Glen.

Relax by the established sweet, dark grapevines and herb gardens surrounding the sparkling, in-ground pool and floodlit, competition-size tennis court.

The home itself boasts an elevated, prized northern aspect, capturing the coastal breezes and has its very own 3,000m2 private rainforest which is home to many species of birds and wildlife.

With a massive 5 bay 90m2 shed (including a shower and toilet), 2 car garage and ample parking spaces (including 2 concrete slabs), you will have plenty of room to store your boat, caravan, prized possessions or run a home-based business.

You will be thoroughly impressed by the brand new, fully equipped kitchen with almost 10 years of warranty remaining and expansive Caesarstone benchtops, high-quality appliances, walk-in pantry, ample storage, gooseneck pull-out mixer, and even your own Hafele wine fridge. There are 3 draws of quality cutlery trays, an inline water filter kit, and a spacious new laundry.

Positioned ideally for those who like to entertain, the kitchen opens effortlessly to the indoor dining and living areas, as well as the paved outdoor alfresco area. One of the best features of the kitchen is its close proximity to the pool and tennis court, enabling you to cook and entertain while keeping an eye on the kids.

Create lasting memories as you “dine in the vines” around the pool in the covered alfresco area, socialising with family and friends. After a refreshing swim in the 10m long and extra-deep swimming pool, take a shower in the Tuscan style pool house with a shower and toilet.

The tennis stars in your family will appreciate the high-fenced, competition size tennis court, complete with full competition lighting. Having just undergone a full electrical refurbishment, your floodlit lighting will ensure no shadows, allowing you to host the ultimate night tennis tournaments!

With a clever split-level design, the top floor consists of your main living areas with soft tones and light-filled spaces to create a luxury feel. The large master suite is complete with an ensuite, walk-in-robe and an opening to a private verandah overlooking the lush rainforest.

A second bedroom with built-in robe completes the upstairs area and could be used as an office or nursery and is conveniently located near the second powder room.

The internal staircase takes you downstairs to the third and fourth bedroom with a bathroom, separate toilet, storage and large living/rumpus. With its own private access, this area offers options for extended family, teenage retreat or dual living arrangements. Additional storage is also available under the home.

Both the downstairs bedrooms are modern and light-filled and overlook a covered patio area with views of the rainforest. One contains a built-in robe, while the other offers a large walk-in robe. The expansive living area offers plenty of scopes to incorporate additional sleeping or games/entertainment/activity area and opens to a private outdoor dining area with rainforest views.

All the hard work is done in this beautifully renovated home, freshly painted inside and out, including the roof! Sustainability lovers will appreciate the 20 solar panels generating 5.2kw, as well as multiple levels of grassed/play areas for the kids or pets. An irrigated veggie patch is planted with an abundance of greens and herbs to make fresh cooking simple.

The property is situated amongst prestigious homes in the most beautiful, leafy rainforest pocket of Forest Glen. Just minutes from major shopping precincts and the Holistic Health hub with transport options to connect easily to the Coast or M1 to Brisbane. Located close to Buderim, Maroochydore, and Mooloolaba, the beautiful beaches of the Sunshine Coast are yours to access.

A short walk to Sunshine Coast Grammar School and within close proximity to Montessori International College, sought after schooling options are within easy reach for the busy family lucky enough to call 14 Anning Road, home.

Rarely does the opportunity arise to own a property with such incredible lifestyle and living options! Call to arrange your inspection today.

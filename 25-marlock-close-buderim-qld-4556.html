Do not miss your opportunity to own a resort style Buderim property nestled in a natural rainforest setting sharing a boundary with Immanuel Lutheran College. Perched high at the top of a quiet cul-de-sac on a huge 2,206m2 allotment, this home is perfectly orientated to make the most of its highly desirable easterly aspect. Rarely does a home of this calibre and location become available, centrally positioned just minutes to Sunshine Coasts world renowned beaches and the Maroochydore CBD.

The sellers have reluctantly downsized now the children have flown the nest to make way for new owners to love this property as much as they have. Imagine a backyard where children never get bored, long swims in the sparkling in-ground pool, exploring the rainforest, kicking the football on the oval sized grassed area that is large enough to put a tennis court, basketball on the driveway or playing in the cubby house and sand pit. One thing is certain you will be impressed by the proportions of the property from the moment you enter through the automated gates, where you will find a long driveway complete with turning circle and parking for all your visitors cars plus room for the caravan, boat and trailer.

The feeling of spaciousness continues on the inside where you will find expansive rooms, high ceilings, skylights, multiple living zones and a deck that wraps around two sides of the home. Spanning two levels, the clever floor plan totalling 500m2 offers space and privacy for all to enjoy.

As you enter through the foyer you will first enter the informal living area providing space for the whole family to come together. Designed to cater for the biggest family gatherings, the centrally located kitchen features South American granite bench tops, breakfast bar along with loads of bench space and storage. Ideally overlooking the sparkling in-ground pool and gardens, the kitchen has a servery making year-round entertaining on the deck a breeze. The large formal dining and living room is a standout offering another space to relax with family and friends complete with fireplace to enjoy in the cooler months.

The bedrooms are more like a retreat, separated from the living zones allowing for quiet separation. The master is complete with access to the deck, a large walk in robe and generous ensuite. As you meander down the light filled corridor you will find two more bedrooms both with views out to the garden, built in robes and serviced by a tastefully renovated main bathroom. An enormous laundry with an abundance of storage adjoins the kitchen for ease of use. This level also has an office and double garage complete with its own storeroom.
On the ground level you will find the fourth bedroom, 3rd bathroom, lounge room, games room and a dance room/gym complete with a mirrored wall. This area offers genuine granny flat potential with its own entry.

Marlock Close is possibly one of Buderim’s best kept secrets with its large blocks, tranquil setting, and central location. Whether you are looking for a private getaway for two, a fantastic family home, dual living, or a property to accommodate a home business this is a rare offering not to be missed.

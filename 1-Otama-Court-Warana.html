Have you been dreaming of a stunning modern home with an outdoor kitchen, gazebo and sparkling in-ground pool just a few blocks from the beach? If so, you need to inspect this recently renovated home that will tick all of the boxes!

Having been completely remodelled with superior quality fittings and fixtures in 2018 and the addition of a second story with a master suite and two extra bathrooms, fourth bedroom and tandem drive through garage, this exquisite beach home is an entertainer’s dream.

The huge designer kitchen has been crafted for the avid chef with solid stone benchtops capable of seating 8 people at the breakfast bar, waterfall edges, quality stainless steel appliances and ample bench space and storage.

With a perfect open plan living area that seamlessly flows to the built-in outdoor wet-bar area, pool and gazebo that boasts a stylish raised timber deck with insulated roofing, built-in timber bar, LED lighting and ceiling fan that is perfectly positioned to look over the sparkling in-ground pool.

Located on the ground floor, two of the bedrooms enjoy their own designer bathroom with a stylish bath and separate toilet. The large laundry is complete with stone bench tops and plenty of cupboard space.

On the recently built upper level, both bedrooms are en-suited with the master including a stylish walk in robe offering soft close draws and padded display draws to showcase your jewellery. With a study nook above and below the stairs, there is plenty of room for study or business needs.

The yard is fully fenced with lawn that is perfect for kids and pets. There is also side access and a convenient drive through tandem garage offering direct access to the back yard. With ceiling fans throughout, LED lighting, screens to windows and doors plus strategically placed louvres to capture the sea breezes, this home is all about ease of living, lifestyle and entertaining in our beautiful coastal climate. The property is perfectly positioned on a corner block within close proximity to schools, shops, the fruit shed, parks, the hospital and Warana’s stunning and dog friendly beach.

There are so many wonderful features of this property that only an inspection will ensure true appreciation. Homes of this calibre don't last long, call now…an inspection is a must.

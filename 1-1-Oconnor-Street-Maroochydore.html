Have you been looking for a luxury ground floor apartment with water views, extremely low body corporate fees, highly prized northern aspect and a huge courtyard for your pets?

Then book in your private inspection to secure this impressive, newly built high-end apartment with stunning river views, privacy and your very own 75m2 exclusive courtyard.

As you enter the apartment, you will love the spacious, light-filled interiors with a timeless palette of neutral colours and natural finishes, high quality Italian tiles, ducted air-conditioning, Miele appliances including a washing machine & dryer, keyless entry, stone bench tops and stylish cabinetry provide.

Floor to ceiling balcony doors offer a seamless blend of indoor and outdoor spaces, maximising the vibrant beauty of the location.
